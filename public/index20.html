A Distinctive Glimpse into Today's Open-Source Ecosystem

This website serves as a collection of my journal entries as I explore and document the constantly changing landscape of open-source technologies. It is a space where I can share insights, experiences, and reflections on the tools and resources that comprise my digital toolbox.

Featuring a distinctive wiki-like structure that mirrors the Vim extension wiki.vim, this simple static website, when used in conjunction with Vim, significantly streamlines the process of content creation. It's the perfect environment for maintaining a journal blog or sharing a curated collection of Jupyter notebooks, all designed for ease and efficiency.

Many of my journal entries are complemented by videos that explain and demonstrate key concepts, providing a richer, more engaging experience for readers.

The content is organized into three categories:

  • Journal: A curated collection of writings and youtube videos, where I delve into the intricacies of technology. From the timeless Vim text editor to the modern versatility of Python, Deno Javascript, Libreoffice, and Vlang, each post is a deep dive into the tools that shape my digital world. Whether you're a seasoned developer or a curious learner, my blog offers insights and tutorials that span a wide spectrum of topics.
  • IPYNB: HTML version of Jupyter Notebooks. These interactive tools not only demonstrate the practical applications of open-source technologies but also serve as a hands-on guide for enthusiasts and professionals alike to experiment, learn, and innovate.
  • Pages: Site's webpages.
